About me

About

Hi, I'm Dan!

I am really passionate about games and music. I play guitar and lyre and I have been creating games as a hobby since I was a child. My first experiences with game development were using Game Maker and before that creating Warcraft 3 maps!
I have a lot of experience using Unity. I have also dabbled with Godot. I can do at least at a basic level all parts of game development - programming, 2d art, 3d modeling, music.
I am a big fan of game jams, I have participated in the Ludum Dare game jam 14 times, first time in 2016 (Ludum Dare 35).
